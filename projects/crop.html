Crop Vision Management

Over View

A conceptual project focused on enhancing agricultural productivity and sustainability. The idea aims to provide farmers with tools and knowledge to monitor crop health, manage resources, and improve yields through a prototype solution.

Features

  • Problem identification through field data and farmer input.
  • Real-time monitoring system to track crop health and growth.
  • Prototype solution integrating weather predictions and smart irrigation logic.
  • Support for fertilizer scheduling and pest control guidance.
  • Focus on sustainable farming practices and community awareness.

Suggested Tech Stack

  • Drones and IoT sensors for crop data collection.
  • AI/ML models for analyzing crop health and predicting outcomes.
  • Mobile/web application for farmer dashboard and insights.
  • Cloud services like Firebase for data storage and access.

What I Learnt

  • Understanding key problems faced in agriculture.
  • Planning scalable, tech-driven solutions for rural issues.
  • Collaborating with others to propose a real-world impact project.
  • Structuring a clear problem statement and building a prototype plan.
